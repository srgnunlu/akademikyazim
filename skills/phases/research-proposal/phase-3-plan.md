# Phase 3 — Work Plan & Timeline
# Faz 3 — Çalışma Planı ve Zaman Çizelgesi

## Gate Rule / Geçiş Kuralı

**EN:** Do NOT proceed to Phase 4 until the committee's central feasibility question is answered: "Can this be completed within the degree time?" The timeline must be realistic, not aspirational. Committees reject proposals where the timeline is impossible.

**TR:** Komitenin merkezi uygulanabilirlik sorusu yanıtlanana kadar Faz 4'e geçme: "Bu derece süresi içinde tamamlanabilir mi?" Zaman çizelgesi idealist değil gerçekçi olmalı. Komiteler, zaman çizelgesi imkânsız olan önerileri reddeder.

---

## Steps / Adımlar

**1. Chapter Outline (Tentative) / Bölüm Taslağı (Geçici)**
A working structure — it WILL evolve, but the committee needs to see you have thought it through:
```
Chapter 1: Introduction
  - Research problem, questions, significance
Chapter 2: Literature Review & Theoretical Framework
  - Major debates, gap, theoretical framework
Chapter 3: Methodology
  - Design, data sources, analytical approach
Chapter 4: [Analysis/Findings — name according to your topic]
Chapter 5: [Analysis/Findings — if needed]
Chapter 6: Discussion & Conclusion
  - Contribution, limitations, future directions
```
Adapt to your discipline — some fields use different structures (article-format dissertations, etc.)
- TR: Çalışan bir yapı — evrilecek, ama komitenin düşündüğünüzü görmesi lazım.

**2. Timeline with Milestones / Kilometre Taşlarıyla Zaman Çizelgesi**
Map the full degree duration with key milestones:

```
Phase          | Start  | End    | Milestone
---------------|--------|--------|------------------------------
Coursework     | [date] | [date] | Required credits completed
Proposal Prep  | [date] | [date] | Proposal draft submitted
Proposal Def.  | [date] | [date] | APPROVAL GATE (this document)
Data Collection| [date] | [date] | Dataset / corpus complete
Analysis       | [date] | [date] | Analysis complete, notes written
Chapter 2 draft| [date] | [date] | Supervisor feedback received
Chapter 3 draft| [date] | [date] | Supervisor feedback received
Chapter 4 draft| [date] | [date] | Supervisor feedback received
Full Draft     | [date] | [date] | Committee review draft
Final Revision | [date] | [date] | Defense-ready manuscript
Thesis Defense | [date] |        | DEGREE COMPLETION
```
Build in buffer time: every project runs late. Add 15-20% buffer.
- TR: Tam derece süresini kilometre taşlarıyla haritalandır. Tampon süre ekle: her proje gecikmeli yürür. %15-20 tampon ekle.

**3. Resources Needed / İhtiyaç Duyulan Kaynaklar**
List what you need to complete the project:
- Library access: specific databases, interlibrary loan needs, special collections access
- Fieldwork funding: travel, accommodation, per diem — does your program fund this?
- Equipment: recording devices, software licenses, computing resources
- Language support: translation, transcription services
- Confirm what your program provides vs. what you must fund independently
- TR: Projeyi tamamlamak için ne gerektiğini listele. Programın sağladığı ile bağımsız olarak finanse etmen gereken arasındaki farkı netleştir.

**4. Risk Identification / Risk Tespiti**
Identify what could delay or endanger the project:

```
Risk                     | Probability | Impact | Mitigation
-------------------------|-------------|--------|------------------
Data access denied       | Medium      | High   | [Alternative source]
Key source unavailable   | Low         | Medium | [Alternative approach]
Scope creep              | High        | High   | [Scope lock agreement]
Personal circumstances   | Low         | High   | [LOA policy known]
Supervisor unavailability| Low         | Medium | [Co-supervisor named]
```
- TR: Projeyi geciktirebilecek veya tehlikeye atacak durumları tespit et.

**5. Realistic vs. Ambitious Test / Gerçekçi ve İdealist Testi**
For each major milestone ask:
- Have students in my program completed similar work in this timeframe? (Base rate)
- What is the minimum viable version of this chapter/analysis? (Floor, not ceiling)
- If I lose one semester to illness or data failure, does the project still complete? (Resilience)

**TR:** Her büyük kilometre taşı için: benzer çalışmalar bu sürede tamamlandı mı? Minimum uygulanabilir versiyon nedir? Bir sömestr kaybolsa proje hâlâ tamamlanır mı?

## Completion Criteria / Tamamlanma Kriterleri

- [ ] Chapter outline written (6+ chapters for dissertation)
- [ ] Full timeline from proposal approval to thesis defense
- [ ] All required resources listed with access confirmed or plan to secure
- [ ] Risk register has at least 4 risks with mitigations
- [ ] Realistic test passed: timeline is feasible, not aspirational
- [ ] work_plan.md and timeline.md saved and ready for committee
