# Phase 1 — Research Design
# Faz 1 — Araştırma Tasarımı

## Gate Rule / Geçiş Kuralı

**EN:** Do NOT proceed to Phase 2 until the methodology is justified (not just described) AND the committee can hold you to the scope statement. Once the proposal is approved, scope is locked.

**TR:** Metodoloji yalnızca tanımlanmakla kalmayıp gerekçelendirilene VE komitenin sizi kapsam beyanına bağlayabileceği açık olana kadar Faz 2'ye geçme. Öneri onaylandıktan sonra kapsam kilitlenir.

---

## Steps / Adımlar

**1. Methodology Choice / Metodoloji Seçimi**
State your methodology AND justify it:
- Qualitative: appropriate when you need depth, context, meaning — interviews, ethnography, discourse analysis, archival research
- Quantitative: appropriate when you need breadth, generalizability, causal inference — surveys, experiments, secondary data analysis
- Mixed methods: appropriate when neither alone is sufficient — explain the integration logic
- Write: "I will use [methodology] because [research questions] require [type of knowledge] that this approach produces."
- TR: Metodolojini seç VE gerekçelendir. "Şunu kullanacağım çünkü araştırma sorularım bu yaklaşımın ürettiği [bilgi türünü] gerektiriyor."

**2. Data Sources / Veri Kaynakları**
Specify what you will study:
- Primary sources: interviews (who? how many? recruitment plan?), experiments (participants, conditions), archives (which collection, access confirmed?)
- Secondary sources: existing datasets (which? access confirmed?), published data, documents
- Confirm access: do not propose a data source you cannot access
- TR: Ne çalışacağını belirt. Birincil ve ikincil kaynakları listele. Erişimi teyit et — erişemeyeceğin veri kaynağını önerme.

**3. Analytical Approach / Analitik Yaklaşım**
Describe how you will analyze data:
- Qualitative: thematic analysis, grounded theory, discourse analysis, hermeneutics — which and why?
- Quantitative: regression, structural equation modeling, machine learning — which and why?
- Connect analysis to research questions: "RQ1 will be addressed through [method] applied to [data]"
- TR: Veriyi nasıl analiz edeceğini açıkla. Analizi araştırma sorularına bağla.

**4. Scope — In and Out / Kapsam — İçinde ve Dışında**
This is critical: committees will hold you to this.
- In scope: [List what you WILL examine]
- Out of scope: [List what you will NOT examine, and why]
- Time period: [Start — End]
- Geographic/population boundary: [Who/what/where is included]
- TR: Komiteniz sizi buna bağlayacak. İçinde ve dışında olanı açıkça listele. Zaman dilimini ve coğrafi/popülasyon sınırını belirle.

**5. Expected Contribution / Beklenen Katkı**
State what this thesis will add to the field:
- Theoretical contribution: new framework, extension of existing theory, critique of dominant approach
- Empirical contribution: new data, new context, new population
- Methodological contribution: novel combination or application of methods
- TR: Bu tezin alana ne katacağını ifade et: teorik katkı, ampirik katkı, metodolojik katkı.

## Feasibility Test / Uygulanabilirlik Testi

Before finalizing, answer these questions honestly:
1. Can I access ALL the data sources listed? (If not, revise scope)
2. Do I have the skills to perform the analysis? (If not, is training built into the plan?)
3. Can this be completed within the degree time limit? (If not, reduce scope)
4. Does my supervisor agree the design is appropriate? (Get this in writing)

**TR:** Sonuçlandırmadan önce bu soruları dürüstçe yanıtla. Hayır yanıtı varsa kapsamı revize et, danışmanından onay al.

---

## Completion Criteria / Tamamlanma Kriterleri

- [ ] Methodology chosen and justified (not just named)
- [ ] All data sources listed with access confirmed
- [ ] Analytical approach linked to each research question
- [ ] Scope written — both IN and OUT of scope stated explicitly
- [ ] Expected contribution stated in at least one category
- [ ] Feasibility test passed (all 4 questions answered affirmatively)
- [ ] research_design.md saved and ready for committee review
