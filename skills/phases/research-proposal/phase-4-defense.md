# Phase 4 — Proposal Defense & Approval
# Faz 4 — Öneri Savunması ve Onayı

## Gate Rule / Geçiş Kuralı

**EN:** Do NOT begin thesis research (Phase 0 of thesis workflow) until approved_proposal.md exists with written committee signatures or formal approval documentation. Verbal approval is not approval.

**TR:** approved_proposal.md yazılı komite imzaları veya resmi onay belgesiyle birlikte mevcut olana kadar tez araştırmasına (tez iş akışının Faz 0'ı) başlama. Sözlü onay, onay değildir.

---

## Steps / Adımlar

**1. Prepare the Defense Presentation / Savunma Sunumunu Hazırla**
Target: 15-20 minutes, leaving 20-30 minutes for committee questions.

Slide structure:
```
Slide 1:  Title, your name, supervisor(s), date
Slide 2:  Research problem — why does this matter?
Slide 3:  The gap — what is specifically unknown?
Slide 4:  Research questions (central + sub-questions)
Slide 5:  Methodology — what and why
Slide 6:  Data sources — what, where, access confirmed
Slide 7:  Theoretical framework
Slide 8:  Chapter outline (visual map)
Slide 9:  Timeline — key milestones
Slide 10: Expected contribution
Slide 11: Known risks and mitigations
Slide 12: Questions / Open discussion
```
Do NOT read from slides. Know your argument well enough to speak to each slide.
- TR: Hedef 15-20 dakika, sorular için 20-30 dakika. Slaytlardan okuma. Her slaydı konuşabilecek kadar iyi bil.

**2. Anticipate Committee Questions / Komite Sorularını Önceden Tahmin Et**
Common challenge areas:

| Challenge | How to Respond |
|---|---|
| "Why this methodology and not X?" | "Because my RQs require [type of evidence] which [chosen method] produces, whereas X would only give me [less appropriate evidence]." |
| "Your scope is too broad." | "I can narrow to [specific reduction]. This would exclude [what] but preserve the core contribution." |
| "How is this different from [existing work]?" | "Study X did [...], but in a different context / using different methods / asking a different question. My contribution is [...]" |
| "Is this feasible in your degree time?" | Point to timeline.md — show the buffer, show the realistic test passed. |
| "What if your data source is unavailable?" | Point to risk_register — show the alternative. |

- TR: Yaygın sorular: metodoloji seçimi, kapsam genişliği, mevcut çalışmadan fark, zaman uygulanabilirliği, veri erişim riski.

**3. Know Your Literature Deeply / Literatürü Derinlemesine Bil**
Committees probe sources you have cited:
- For every source in literature_foundation.md: be prepared to explain its argument, its relevance, its limitations
- You will be asked: "Have you read [author]?" — only cite what you have actually read
- If asked about a source you haven't read: "I'm familiar with the main argument but haven't done a close reading — I'll add that before data collection."
- TR: Komiteler atıf yaptığın kaynakları sorgular. Sadece gerçekten okuduğun şeyleri kaynak göster.

**4. After Approval: Get It in Writing / Onay Sonrası: Yazıya Dök**
Immediately after approval:
- Request the signed approval form or committee approval email
- Create approved_proposal.md capturing exactly what was approved:
```
Approved_Proposal.md
Date of Approval: [Date]
Committee Members: [Names + signatures or email confirmation]
Approved Scope:
  RQ1 (Central): [exact wording]
  RQ2: [exact wording]
  Methodology: [exactly as approved]
  Data sources: [exactly as approved]
  Time period: [exactly as approved]
  Explicitly excluded: [what is out of scope]
Required Revisions (if any): [List — see revision_notes.md]
Revision Deadline: [Date]
```
This document is the scope agreement. Any significant change requires committee re-approval.
- TR: Hemen onaydan sonra imzalı onay formunu veya komite onay e-postasını iste. Kapsam anlaşmasını yaz.

**5. Post-Approval Revisions / Onay Sonrası Revizyonlar**
Committees often approve with minor required revisions:
- Document every required revision in revision_notes.md
- Assign completion date to each revision
- Confirm with supervisor when revisions are satisfactory
- Do NOT begin data collection until all required revisions are signed off

**TR:** Komiteler çoğunlukla küçük gerekli revizyonlarla onay verir. Her gerekli revizyonu belgele. Tüm gerekli revizyonlar onaylanana kadar veri toplamaya başlama.

## Output Checklist / Çıktı Kontrol Listesi

```
[ ] defense_slides.pdf — 10-12 slides, 15-20 min structure
[ ] Defense delivered successfully
[ ] Written committee approval received (signed form OR official email)
[ ] approved_proposal.md created with exact scope language
[ ] revision_notes.md created (even if "No revisions required")
[ ] All required revisions completed and confirmed
[ ] Supervisor confirms: ready to begin thesis Phase 0
```

---

## Gate to Thesis Workflow / Tez İş Akışına Geçiş Kapısı

**EN:** When approved_proposal.md is complete and all revisions confirmed, you may begin the thesis workflow. The approved proposal defines your scope — do not drift from it without committee discussion.

**TR:** approved_proposal.md tamamlandığında ve tüm revizyonlar onaylandığında tez iş akışına başlayabilirsin. Onaylı öneri kapsamını tanımlar — komite tartışması olmadan bu kapsamdan sapma.

---

## Completion Criteria / Tamamlanma Kriterleri

- [ ] Presentation prepared (10-12 slides, practice run completed)
- [ ] Committee questions anticipated and responses rehearsed
- [ ] Literature knowledge sufficient to defend every citation
- [ ] Defense presented
- [ ] Written approval secured (not verbal)
- [ ] approved_proposal.md complete with exact scope language
- [ ] revision_notes.md complete
- [ ] All required revisions confirmed by supervisor
