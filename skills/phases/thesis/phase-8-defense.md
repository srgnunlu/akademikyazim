# Phase 8 — Defense Preparation / Faz 8 — Savunma Hazırlığı

## Purpose / Amaç

**EN:** A thesis defense is an oral examination of both the document and the researcher. This phase ensures you are intellectually and procedurally ready to present your work, handle critique, and manage committee dynamics.

**TR:** Tez savunması, hem belgenin hem de araştırmacının sözlü sınavıdır. Bu faz, çalışmanızı sunmaya, eleştirileri ele almaya ve jüri dinamiklerini yönetmeye entelektüel ve prosedürel olarak hazır olmanızı sağlar.

---

## Steps / Adımlar

**1. Committee Profiling / Jüri Profili Çıkarma**
- Analyze the past work of your committee members.
- What methodologies do they favor? What theoretical camps do they belong to?
- Anticipate at least one specific question from each member based on their background.

**2. Presentation Design (The 20-Minute Narrative) / Sunum Tasarımı**
- Do NOT read your thesis. The committee has already read it.
- Focus on the narrative arc: The Problem → The Gap → Your Approach → Key Findings → The Contribution.
- Create `defense_slides.md` outlining the content for a 15-20 minute presentation.

**3. The 'Defense Armor' Q&A / Savunma Zırhı Soru-Cevap**
- Draft responses to the 5 most difficult questions (methodological weaknesses, alternative interpretations, scope limitations).
- Prepare the "Contribution Elevator Pitch" (be able to state your thesis's value in 30 seconds).

**4. Mock Defense / Prova Savunma**
- Present to peers or colleagues who will ask hostile questions.
- Record the session and note where you hesitated.
