# Phase 5 — Review & Submission
# Faz 5 — İnceleme ve Gönderim

## Gate Rule / Geçiş Kuralı

**EN:** Do NOT submit until: (1) an independent internal reader has reviewed it, (2) institutional sign-off is confirmed, and (3) every funder requirement on the compliance checklist is ticked. Never submit in the last hour.

**TR:** Şunlar olmadan gönderme: (1) bağımsız bir iç okuyucu inceledi, (2) kurumsal onay alındı, (3) uyum kontrol listesindeki her fon kaynağı gereksinimi işaretlendi. Son saatte asla gönderme.

---

## Steps / Adımlar

**1. Internal Review / İç İnceleme**
- Find a colleague unfamiliar with your project — ideally from a neighboring but not identical field
- Ask them: "Can you understand what we're doing, why it matters, and why we can do it — in 10 minutes?"
- Their confusion = your problem, not their limitation
- Key questions to ask the reviewer:
  - Is the innovation claim clear?
  - Is the methodology convincing?
  - Does the budget seem proportionate?
- TR: Projenizi tanımayan bir meslektaş bulun. Onlara 3 soru sor. Onların kafa karışıklığı senin problemin.

**2. Institutional Sign-Off / Kurumsal Onay**
Most funders require institutional (not individual) submission:
- Identify your institution's grants office and their internal deadline (often 5–10 days before funder deadline)
- Prepare required institutional documents: cover letter, institutional eligibility confirmation, overhead rate letter, co-PI authorizations
- Budget must be approved by finance office
- TR: Fon kaynakları genellikle bireysel değil kurumsal başvuru ister. Kurumunuzun iç son teslim tarihini öğrenin.

**3. Compliance Check / Uyum Kontrolü**
Work through every funder requirement from Phase 0:

```
COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST
[ ] Page limit not exceeded (count every page including references)
[ ] Font and margin requirements met
[ ] All mandatory sections present (check call guidelines)
[ ] CVs included (correct format, correct length per funder rules)
[ ] Letters of support attached (signed, dated, addressed correctly)
[ ] Ethics documentation attached (if required at submission stage)
[ ] Data management plan attached (if required)
[ ] Budget within cap
[ ] Budget form in funder's required format
[ ] Co-PI/collaborator authorizations confirmed
[ ] Institutional authorization confirmed
[ ] All files in required format (PDF, Word, specific naming convention)
```

**TR:** Faz 0'dan gelen her fon kaynağı gereksinimini tek tek işaretle.

**4. Portal Submission / Portal Gönderimi**
- Test the submission portal at least one week before the deadline (portals crash on deadline day)
- Upload and check each file renders correctly in the portal preview
- Submit with at least 24 hours to spare — never in the last hour
- Save the submission confirmation number and timestamp
- TR: Portalı en az bir hafta önce test et. En az 24 saat öncesinde gönder. Onay numarasını kaydet.

**5. Post-Submission Record / Gönderim Sonrası Kayıt**

Create submission_confirmation.md:
```
Submission Date: [Date + Time]
Confirmation Number: [Portal reference ID]
Funder: [Name]
Call: [Title + Reference]
Expected Decision Date: [Date]
Decision Notification Method: [Email / Portal / Other]
Reviewer Feedback: [Will be available? How to request?]
Next Steps if Rejected: [Resubmit same call / Different call / Archive]
```

---

## If Rejected / Red Gelirse

**EN:** Rejection is the norm, not the exception — most calls have 10–30% success rates.
1. Request reviewer feedback if the funder provides it
2. Read feedback carefully; identify the 2 most substantive criticisms
3. Decide: resubmit to same call (next round), revise for different call, or archive
4. Do NOT resubmit without addressing reviewer criticisms — reviewers remember weak proposals

**TR:** Red norm, istisna değildir — çoğu çağrının başarı oranı %10-30'dur. Hakem geri bildirimini iste. En önemli 2 eleştiriyi tespit et. Eleştirileri ele almadan yeniden gönderme.

---

## Completion Criteria / Tamamlanma Kriterleri

- [ ] Internal review completed with at least one independent reader
- [ ] Institutional sign-off confirmed (not assumed)
- [ ] Full compliance checklist completed — no unchecked items
- [ ] Portal tested before deadline day
- [ ] Submitted at least 24 hours before deadline
- [ ] submission_confirmation.md created with all fields
- [ ] Post-rejection plan documented regardless of outcome
