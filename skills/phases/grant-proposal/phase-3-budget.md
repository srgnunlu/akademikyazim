# Phase 3 — Budget Justification
# Faz 3 — Bütçe Gerekçesi

## Gate Rule / Geçiş Kuralı

**EN:** Do NOT proceed to Phase 4 until every budget line item has a one-sentence justification and the total falls within the call's budget cap. "Misc expenses" without explanation is a rejection risk.

**TR:** Her bütçe kalemi için bir cümlelik gerekçe yazılana ve toplam çağrının bütçe tavanı içinde kalana kadar Faz 4'e geçme. Açıklamasız "çeşitli giderler" kaydı ret riskidir.

---

## Core Principle / Temel İlke

**EN:** A budget is a narrative argument about what resources are necessary to deliver the science. Reviewers ask: "Is this realistic? Is it efficient? Is every item justified?"

**TR:** Bütçe, bilimi üretmek için hangi kaynakların gerekli olduğuna dair bir anlatı argümanıdır. Hakemler sorar: "Bu gerçekçi mi? Verimli mi? Her kalem gerekçeli mi?"

---

## Steps / Adımlar

**1. Personnel Costs / Personel Maliyetleri**
Justify every FTE:
- PI buy-out: how many months, at what percentage — why is this PI involvement level required?
- Postdoc(s): why 24 months vs. 12? What specific tasks require postdoc-level expertise?
- PhD students: are they doing original data collection, computation, fieldwork?
- Research assistant / technician: what routine tasks do they perform?
- TR: Her FTE için gerekçe yaz. Neden bu kadar ay? Bu görevler için neden bu düzeyde uzmanlık gerekiyor?

**2. Equipment / Ekipman**
Justify necessity:
- "We request [equipment] because [specific task in WP-X] requires [capability] that is not available in existing institutional infrastructure."
- For software licenses: name the software, the tasks, the number of users, duration
- TR: Her ekipman için zorunluluk gerekçesi: mevcut kurumsal altyapıda neden mevcut değil?

**3. Travel / Seyahat**
Justify each travel item:
- Conference presentations: which conference, how many attendees, why this venue for dissemination?
- Collaboration visits: which partner institution, what collaborative work occurs during the visit?
- Fieldwork: where, duration, what data is collected that cannot be collected remotely?
- TR: Her seyahat kalemi için: hangi konferans/kurum, ne amaçla, uzaktan yapılamaz mı?

**4. Indirect Costs / Genel Giderler (Overhead)**
- Know your institution's negotiated overhead rate (typically 20–60% of direct costs depending on funder rules)
- Some funders cap overhead (e.g., Horizon Europe: 25% flat rate)
- State the rate and its source (institutional negotiated rate document)
- TR: Kurumunun müzakere edilmiş genel gider oranını bil. Fon kaynağının tavan kurallarını kontrol et.

**5. Value for Money / Para Karşılığı Değer**
Write a brief paragraph (3-5 sentences) arguing the total budget is proportionate to the expected outputs:
- "For [total amount], this project will produce [X publications], [Y datasets], [Z trained researchers], and [W policy impact]."
- TR: Toplam bütçenin beklenen çıktılarla orantılı olduğunu savunan kısa bir paragraf yaz.

## Common Errors to Avoid / Kaçınılacak Yaygın Hatalar

- Requesting equipment already owned by your institution without justification of why yours is insufficient
- Under-budgeting personnel (makes reviewers doubt feasibility)
- Over-budgeting travel relative to research scope (signals poor planning)
- Missing indirect costs (will be flagged or auto-added by institution)
- Round numbers without justification ($10,000 for "supplies") — be specific

---

## Completion Criteria / Tamamlanma Kriterleri

- [ ] Every personnel FTE justified with specific task linkage to WPs
- [ ] Every equipment item justified (necessity + unavailability argument)
- [ ] Every travel item justified (purpose + dissemination/collaboration link)
- [ ] Indirect costs calculated at correct institutional rate
- [ ] Total within call budget cap
- [ ] Value for money paragraph written
- [ ] Budget table consistent with WP resource list from Phase 2
