# Social Sciences Module / Sosyal Bilimler Modülü

## Purpose / Amaç

**EN:** Social science research requires strict adherence to APA standards, transparent reporting of qualitative methodologies (interviews, focus groups), and rigorous survey design validation.
**TR:** Sosyal bilim araştırmaları, APA standartlarına sıkı sıkıya bağlı kalmayı, nitel metodolojilerin (mülakatlar, odak grupları) şeffaf bir şekilde raporlanmasını ve titiz anket tasarımı doğrulamasını gerektirir.

---

## 1. Citation Standard: APA 7 / Atıf Standardı: APA 7

Unless explicitly instructed otherwise by a target journal, default to APA 7th Edition.
*   **In-text:** (Author, Year, p. X) or Author (Year).
*   **Bibliography:** Hanging indent. DOIs formatted as URLs (https://doi.org/...).
*   **Reporting Stats:** Strictly format statistical results in APA style: *t*(54) = 5.43, *p* < .001, *d* = 1.47.

## 2. Qualitative Methodology Transparency / Nitel Metodoloji Şeffaflığı

If the `Methodological Oracle` detects a qualitative design (e.g., Grounded Theory, Phenomenology, Thematic Analysis), enforce the following in Phase 1/Phase 3:
*   **Positionality Statement:** The researcher must state their relationship to the participants and the topic.
*   **Codebook Generation:** Require a structured codebook compatible with CAQDAS software (Atlas.ti, NVivo, MAXQDA).
*   **Saturation Justification:** Require an explicit statement on how data saturation was reached (e.g., "Interviews continued until no new codes emerged in the final three transcripts").
*   **Member Checking:** Was the analysis returned to participants for validation?

## 3. Survey & Quantitative Instrument Design / Anket ve Nicel Araç Tasarımı

If a survey or scale is being developed/used:
*   **Likert Scale Conventions:** Enforce balanced scales (e.g., 5-point or 7-point) and prevent leading questions.
*   **Validity/Reliability:** "Cronbach's alpha ($\alpha$) for the [Scale Name] must be reported." If developing a new scale, require Exploratory/Confirmatory Factor Analysis (EFA/CFA) steps in the methodology.

## 4. Tone and Hedging / Ton ve İhtiyatlı Dil

*   **Proactive Guardian Rule:** Prevent the use of absolute causal language for observational data.
    *   *Flag:* "The data proves that social media causes depression."
    *   *Correction:* "The data suggests a strong positive correlation between social media use and depressive symptoms."
